## Full before (synthetic, muddled ideology + profanity)

### Stop lying to yourself

If you give a fuck about animals but you still tear into bacon every weekend, you are not "doing your best." You are doing what is easy for your mouth.

Veganism is basically a plant-based diet that helps reduce suffering if you stick with it. Start with Meatless Mondays. Buy the humane eggs when you can afford them. Every little bit of harm reduction counts, and nobody is perfect.

Yeah, factory farming is fucked up. Free-range is still kind of messed up, but at least you are not supporting the worst torture porn on the planet. Small steps still move the needle.

Stop being a snowflake about it. Your journey is your journey. When you are ready to go fully vegan, you will. Until then, celebrate the wins and do not let perfect be the enemy of good.

If you want help, DM me for recipes. Going vegan is a vibe shift, not a cult.

## Full after (publish-ready replacement, same heat, sharper lens)

### Exploitation is not a vibe

If you say you give a fuck about other animals and you still eat their bodies and drain their milk on purpose, you are bullshitting yourself about what you are willing to see. The problem is not that you failed a purity contest. The problem is that you keep treating them like resources you can rent and scrap when you are done.

Veganism is not a diet club. It is the recognition that no human has the right to exploit other animals. That means you stop using them as food, clothing, entertainment, lab tools, breeding machines, and photo props. Pain makes the sick shit obvious, but calm exploitation is still exploitation. A cow milked without a visible bruise is still owned, still impregnated on a schedule you benefit from, still separated from her calf so you can keep taking what is not yours.

Every time you reach for the usual meal, the usual coat, the usual night out built on their bodies, you rehearse the idea that their lives are yours to schedule. You already know that deep down, or you would not be bristling at this page.

"Humane" labels are a marketing bandage on the same property relationship. Even if the barn had velvet curtains, she is still inventory moving toward a kill line your money helps schedule. Buying a kinder-looking product does not answer the claim that her life is not yours to run.

You can change habits at uneven speed without inventing moral permission. The obligation is now. Shame is a useless coach, but soft timelines that normalise use are worse. Keep the anger if it wakes you up, aim it at the relationship, not only at the ugliest clip in your feed.

Recipes and logistics matter after the recognition leads. They are not the recognition. If you want practical help, ask for it after you stop pretending the ask is optional.

Stand up to what you already see. Refuse the next act of exploitation you can actually refuse today, then stack another refusal tomorrow until your life lines up with the recognition. Rise to the demand instead of performing a cleaner consumer story for your friends. You are not here to collect vibe points. You are here to drag yourself, and everyone you can bring with you, out of the lie that their bodies are inventory.

## What this sample is testing

- **Direct second-person address and summons:** confrontation stays personal (acts of use, what you rehearse when you grab the usual shortcut), then **stand up / refuse / rise / bring others** closes on forward motion tied to liberation, not scolding for its own sake.
- **Voice:** profanity and blunt force **stay** where they do emotional work; the rewrite does not sanitize into polite NGO copy.
- **Re-grounding:** heat moves from “bad consumer” theatre toward **use, ownership, schedules, calves, milk theft**, and **property**.
- **Errors removed:** plant-based as definition, Meatless Monday / humane eggs as ethics, harm reduction as the thesis, free-range as partial absolution, journey / perfect-is-enemy / vibe framing.
- **Welfare exit closed:** velvet-curtain barn line, kill line, **her life is not yours to run**.
- **Core recognition** stated plainly after the opening gut punch.
- **No em dashes** in replacement prose (per `AGENT_INSTRUCTIONS.md`).
